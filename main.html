About Adele Collections

Adele Collections is a unique luxury brand that brings the artistry of Van Gogh to the world of high-end fashion and accessories. Inspired by the vibrant colors and emotive brushstrokes of the iconic painter, we create pieces that are not just accessories, but wearable art.

Our mission is to make luxury accessible to the discerning Indian market. We proudly bring together a curated selection of international luxury brands that were previously unavailable in India, alongside our own Van Gogh-inspired creations.

At Adele Collections, we believe that true luxury lies in the intersection of art, craftsmanship, and personal expression. Each piece in our collection tells a story, bridging the gap between classical art and contemporary fashion.
